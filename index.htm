About Us

Strings of Kolkata is a Western classical music band dedicated to bringing the elegance and depth of string music to audiences in Kolkata and beyond. The ensemble features talented musicians performing a diverse repertoire, from Baroque to contemporary compositions. Our goal is to promote Western classical music in India through live performances, collaborations, and innovative projects.
