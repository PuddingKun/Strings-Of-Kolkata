About Strings of Kolkata

Strings of Kolkata is a Western classical music band dedicated to bringing the elegance and depth of string music to audiences in Kolkata and beyond. Founded by a group of passionate musicians, the ensemble is known for its musical storytelling, technical finesse, and emotionally resonant performances.

We perform a diverse repertoire that spans the Baroque, Classical, and Romantic eras, and are constantly exploring ways to bring string music into contemporary spaces. Our mission is to promote and popularize Western classical music in India through live concerts, educational initiatives, and collaborations with artists across genres.

Our Vision

To be a cultural beacon for Western classical music in India and inspire the next generation of musicians and music lovers.

Our Ensemble

We adapt our performances based on the occasion — performing as a soloist, duo, trio, quartet, or full ensemble. Whether it's an intimate gathering or a grand concert hall, our goal remains the same: to move hearts through music.
